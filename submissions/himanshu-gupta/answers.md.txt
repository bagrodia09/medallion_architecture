# Answers

| Question | Answer | Brief Explanation |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| Q1 | B | The Bronze layer ingests and stores raw, unmodified data exactly as received from source systems. |
| Q2 | C | The Silver layer applies data quality rules such as deduplication, type casting, validation, etc. |
| Q3 | C | The Gold layer contains curated, business-ready datasets such as aggregates, KPIs, and dimensional views. |
| Q4 | B | Bronze data is not updated or corrected, hence, issues are handled downstream so the raw source of truth is always preserved. |
| Q5 | C | Schema flexibility is highest in Bronze, enforced and refined in Silver, and controlled in Gold to protect business use-case. |
